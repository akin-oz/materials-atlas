Those external works remain owned by their respective authors, publishers, institutions, or maintainers.

Readers should follow the license, copyright, and citation requirements of each external work.

# Curated Resources

A curated resource entry is part of Materials Atlas.

The original resource it points to is not.

For example:

- the Materials Atlas description, selection rationale, categorization, and learning context are original repository content
- the linked paper, book, software package, dataset, video, or website remains governed by its own terms

This distinction matters for attribution.

When citing or reusing an external resource, cite the external source directly.

When citing or reusing the Materials Atlas synthesis around that resource, cite Materials Atlas as well.

# Attribution

Reuse is encouraged.

Attribution should preserve the connection between the reused material and Materials Atlas.

A suitable attribution is:

```text
Adapted from Materials Atlas by Akin Oztorun, licensed under CC BY 4.0.
https://github.com/akin-oz/materials-atlas
```

If you substantially modify the material, indicate that changes were made.
