# Researchers

This index maps researchers whose work helps define Computational Materials Science, Materials Informatics, AI for Materials, or the software and data infrastructure around those fields.

It is not a biography directory. Researchers are included to help readers understand influence, intellectual lineage, infrastructure, and active research directions.

## Selection Philosophy

A researcher may be included when their work clearly shapes one or more domains through methods, software, datasets, institutions, review work, or field-defining research programs.

Inclusion should explain why the person matters to the map. It should not summarize a career or rank people.

Use lightweight relationships:

- Associated domains: conceptual areas shaped by the researcher.
- Associated infrastructure: software, datasets, workflows, or institutions connected to the work.
- Leads to: the next concept a reader should inspect.

## Inclusion Criteria

Future additions should satisfy at least one of these conditions:

- the researcher created or led infrastructure used by the field
- the researcher introduced methods that shaped a domain
- the researcher helped define a major research direction
- the researcher connects multiple domains in a way that improves the map
- the researcher's work provides a useful entry point into a field

If the entry would be only a short biography, do not add it.
